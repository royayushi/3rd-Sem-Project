Route Location

Route Location allows you to see the real-time location of your bus stops on a map. Once you select your desired route from the dropdown, you will be able to see your bus informations like bus number, origin, destination, bus stops, bus fare. Along with a map!

That's correct, the map will display all of the location points the bus passes through. The blue location symbol represents the origin, the red one represents the destination, and the yellow ones represent the stops your bus makes.

Don't scroll yet, as there's more our map presents. Like your ETA, and the feature to click on the location symbols and see the location name and coordinates.
